ABOUT

Ever since the inception of AK Group in 1984 in the Palakkad of Kerala a small clinic, the focus to provide high-quality medical services to its patients continued even when the hospital was built on a small scale with minimum resources. The vision of group founder was simple and straightforward; to bring international standards of health care within the reach of common man in the Kerala as envisaged by him.


It was possible only by the trust that people bestowed upon us and today AK Group has grown into a conglomerate that is serving humankind across the Kerala, with Two multi-specialty hospitals, 20 satellite medical centers in the peripheral areas of Kerala along with 35 Pharmacies providing high quality services and products to its patients. The group also operates a line of Ophthalmology Centers and Optical shops sprouting under the brand name Unique AK Ophthalmology Medical Centers in Kerala.
